Bhagavad Gita Chapter 2, Sloka 16

नासतो विद्यते भावो नाभावो विद्यते सतः।
उभयोरपि दृष्टोऽन्तस्त्वनयोस्तत्त्वदर्शिभिः॥

Translation

The unreal has no existence; the real never ceases to be; the seers of truth have concluded the same about both.

Commentary

This verse explains the eternal nature of the soul and the temporary nature of the physical body. The wise understand this distinction and are not deluded by the temporary appearances of the material world.
