Bhagavad Gita - Chapter 2, Sloka 12

देहिनोऽस्मिन्यथा देहे कौमारं यौवनं जरा। तथा देहान्तरप्राप्तिर्धीरस्तत्र न मुह्यति॥

Translation: Just as the boyhood, youth, and old age come to the embodied Soul in this body, in the same manner, death comes to the embodied Soul. The wise man is not deluded by that.
